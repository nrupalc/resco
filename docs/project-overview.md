## What is RESCO?

RESCO (Renewable Energy Service Company) - a business model where the company installs, owns, and operates solar panel systems on a customer's premises and sells the generated electricity back to them under a long-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) at rates below grid tariffs.

## Business Model

- Install solar panels on residential apartment building rooftops at the company's cost
- Sell generated electricity to residents via long-term PPAs at ~20-25% below their current grid tariffs
- Retain ownership of the installation even after breakeven (unlike competitors like Volt who hand over)
- Revenue comes from the spread between generation cost and PPA rate
- Target: high-consumption apartment societies (40K+ monthly bills, 800+ units/month)

### Sweat Equity Buy-in (Kalyan & Cnu)

- Kalyan and Cnu each hold 10% equity earned through on-ground work (sweat equity)
- They have **3 years** to purchase their shares at **original investment pricing** (not appreciated value)
- Buy-in amount is based on actual capital deployed across all projects
- Example: if 5 projects deployed at total cost of 32.5L, each buys in at 10% = **3.25L each**
- When they buy in, the money flows back to the 3 cash investors (43.75/43.75/12.5) as capital reimbursement
- After buy-in, each partner's net investment matches their equity percentage

### Salary & Profit Policy

- **No salary** for anyone until the buy-in period (3 years) is complete
- **No profit withdrawals** - all profits are reinvested into the company to fund growth and new projects
- Salary and profit distribution policies to be revisited after the buy-in period ends

## Key Risks Identified

1. **Regulatory** - Net metering policy changes, TGREDCO approval delays, tariff structure changes
2. **Customer/Revenue** - Society committee turnover, payment defaults, consumption-capped revenue
3. **Financial** - High leverage vulnerability, interest rate exposure, working capital strain
4. **Technical** - Rooftop structural issues, generation underperformance, panel degradation
5. **Competitive** - Larger RESCO players (like Volt) entering residential market, technology disruption
6. **Legal** - PPA enforceability over 25 years, liability for roof/electrical damage
7. **Scaling** - Each society is unique, operational complexity grows non-linearly

Full risk analysis with mitigations documented separately.
